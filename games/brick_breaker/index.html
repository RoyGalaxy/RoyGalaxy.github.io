Controls


30% of the screen size from the bottom side is reserved for controls.
The left side will move the paddle to the left side. Similarly the right side will move the paddle to right side. Tap the controll buttons to move the paddle only in a small amount, press and hold to keep moving.

Rules


  • Every User gets 3 lives.
  • Whenever the ball touches bottom of the screen one life is lost
  • Breaking each brick will give you 10 points
  • There are a total of 3 levels in the game
  • Every level has one extra row and the speed of ball is increased
  • Try hitting two bricks at once. This will prevent the ball from going back down
